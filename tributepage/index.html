Sushant Singh Rajput

1986 - 2020

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor who is best known for his work in Hindi cinema. His sucessfull Bollywood movies include M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story (2016), Kedarnath (2018) and Chhichhore (2019). He also received a Screen Award and was nominated for the Filmfare Awards on two occasions. Sushant Singh Rajpt was deeply interested in astrophysics and won the National Olympiad in Physics. He secured an admission in the Delhi College of Engineering (later renamed to Delhi Technological University) to pursue a Bachelor of Engineering degree in mechanical engineering.
